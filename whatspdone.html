Environmental Sustainability in SP

SP has pledged to become a green campus, reduce its environmental impact and nurture people of sustanability.

If you take a walk around the campus, it is evident that it has come to fruition.

SP's campus is home to roughly 230 different species of plants! The presence of nature allows us to feel relaxed and helps calm us down when we are stressed. It also improves the quality of our air.

Facilities on campus are equipped with smart sensors that monitors motion and air quality. For example, lights along T7's corridor have motion sensors. This allows the lights to be turnt on only when it is necessary.

Common Core Curriculum(CCC) modules have been introduced into some diplomas. They are structured around the SG Green Plan and United Nations sustanability Development Goals(UNSDGs).

Allowing students to be versed with global issues and inter-disciplinary learning.
