Environmental Sustainability in SP

SP has pledged to become a green campus, reduce its environmental impact and nurture people of sustanability.


Here are some of the efforts that Singapore Polytechnic have taken for enviromental sustainability.

SP's campus is home to roughly 230 different species of plants! The presence of nature allows us to feel relaxed and helps calm us down when we are stressed. It also improves the quality of our air.

Majority of the facilities on campus are equipped with smart sensors that monitors motion and air quality. For example, lights along T7's corridor have motion sensors. This allows the lights to be turnt on only when it is necessary.

Common Core Curriculum(CCC) modules have been introduced into some diplomas. They are structured around the SG Green Plan and United Nations sustanability Development Goals(UNSDGs). Within these modules, students will think critically about real-world issues Allowing students to be versed with global issues and inter-disciplinary learning.

SP houses 4 Green Mark Platinum Buildings.
T18A, T18B, T3B and the SP Sports Arena.
Green mark's building rating system evaluates its environmental impact and performance. There are on-going efforts are being made to certify all 22 buildings in SP by 2025.
